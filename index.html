Privacy Policy – Neon Kitty Blast

Effective Date: April 2026

Neon Kitty Blast (“the Game”) is a family-friendly twin-stick shooter designed for Android devices. The Game allows players to customize their character and choose a nickname.

Online Features

The Game supports optional online multiplayer via direct peer-to-peer (IP-based) connection. This feature is used solely to allow players to connect with each other for gameplay.

The Game does not operate or use any developer-controlled servers. All connections are established directly between players' devices.

Data Collection and Usage

The Game does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The Game uses standard Android network permissions (INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE) required for online multiplayer functionality. As part of establishing a peer-to-peer connection, a player’s IP address may be processed temporarily by the player’s device.

This information is:

  • not collected by the developer
  • not stored
  • not tracked
  • not shared with any third parties
  • not transmitted to developer-controlled servers

All network communication occurs directly between players' devices without involvement of the developer. The developer does not have access to any connection data.

Payments and Monetization

Neon Kitty Blast is a paid application. The Game does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, advertisements, or any additional monetization mechanisms.

Content and Safety

The Game is designed to be family-friendly. It does not contain blood, gore, explicit violence, or sensitive or health-related content.

Children’s Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under applicable laws. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted to the developer, the Game is designed to comply with child privacy regulations such as COPPA and GDPR-K.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
