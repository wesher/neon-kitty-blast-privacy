Privacy Policy – Neon Kitty Blast

Neon Kitty Blast is a family-friendly twin-stick shooter game designed for Android devices. The game allows players to customize their character and choose a nickname.

Online Features

The game supports optional online multiplayer via direct peer-to-peer (IP-based) connection. This feature is used solely to allow players to connect with each other for gameplay.

The game does not operate its own servers. Connections are established directly between players.

Data Collection and Usage

Neon Kitty Blast does not collect, store, or share personal data.

However, the game uses standard network functionality (INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permissions) required for online multiplayer. As part of this functionality, a player's IP address may be temporarily used to establish a direct connection between players.

This information is:

  • not stored
  • not tracked
  • not shared with the developer or third parties
  • not transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer

All connections occur directly between players' devices.

Payments and Monetization

Neon Kitty Blast is a paid game. It does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, advertisements, or any additional monetization.

Content and Safety

Neon Kitty Blast is designed to be family-friendly. It contains no blood, gore, or explicit violence, and does not include sensitive or health-related content.

Children’s Privacy

Neon Kitty Blast does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the game does not store or transmit personal data to the developer, it is designed to comply with applicable child privacy regulations.
